# ShiftConnect User Guide

## Overview

ShiftConnect is a shift management platform that helps you view available shifts, express interest, and receive notifications about scheduling updates. This guide covers everything you need to know as an employee.

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open ShiftConnect in your web browser
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click "Login"

If you don't have login credentials, contact your supervisor to enable web access for your account.

---

## Viewing Available Shifts

### From the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard with:
- **Available Shifts** - Open shifts you can express interest in
- **Your Upcoming Shifts** - Shifts you've been assigned to

### Shift Details

Each shift card shows:
- **Position** - The role required (e.g., DSP, RN, CNA)
- **Area** - The department or service area
- **Date and Time** - When the shift starts and ends
- **Location** - Where to report
- **Bonus** - Extra pay offered (shown in green if available)
- **Status** - Available, Claimed, or Expired

### Expressing Interest

1. Find an available shift
2. Click "I'm Interested" or "View Details"
3. Confirm your interest

Your supervisor will be notified and may assign you to the shift.

---

## SMS Commands

You can interact with ShiftConnect via text message. Reply to any ShiftConnect message with these commands:

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `YES` | Express interest in the most recent shift notification |
| `YES [CODE]` | Express interest in a specific shift (e.g., `YES ABC123`) |
| `NO` | Decline a shift offer |
| `SHIFTS` | View all available shifts you're eligible for |
| `STATUS` | Check your assigned shifts and pending interests |
| `CONFIRM` | Confirm an assigned shift |
| `CANCEL` | Cancel a shift or withdraw interest |
| `HELP` | Show available commands |
| `STOP` | Opt out of SMS notifications |
| `START` | Opt back in to SMS notifications |

### Using Shift Codes

When you receive a shift notification, it includes a 6-character code (e.g., `ABC123`). Use this code to respond to specific shifts:

```
YES ABC123
```

This is helpful when you receive multiple shift notifications and want to respond to a specific one.

---

## Notifications

### Types of Notifications

- **New Shift Available** - When a shift matching your position is posted
- **Shift Confirmation** - When you're assigned to a shift
- **Shift Reminder** - Before your shift starts
- **Shift Cancelled** - If a shift you're assigned to is cancelled

### Managing Notifications

- Reply `STOP` to opt out of SMS notifications
- Reply `START` to opt back in
- Contact your supervisor to update notification preferences

---

## Checking Your Schedule

### Via SMS

Text `STATUS` to see:
- Your upcoming assigned shifts
- Shifts where you've expressed interest

### Via Web

1. Log in to ShiftConnect
2. View your dashboard for upcoming shifts

---

## Tips

- Respond quickly to shift notifications - popular shifts fill fast
- Use shift codes when responding to avoid confusion
- Keep your phone number updated with your supervisor
- Check the app regularly for new opportunities

---

## Need Help?

Contact your supervisor or facility administrator for assistance with:
- Account access issues
- Shift assignment questions
- Schedule conflicts
- Updating your contact information
